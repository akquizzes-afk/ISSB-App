Picture Story Test

Picture Story Test Instructions

1
You will be shown 4 images, each for 30 seconds.
2
After each image, you'll have 3 minutes and 20 seconds to write a story based on what you saw.
3
Look at each image carefully and try to understand what's happening in the scene.
4
When writing your story, be creative and include details about the characters, setting, and plot.
5
Describe what might have happened before the scene, what's happening now, and what might happen next.
6
There are no right or wrong answers. Write what comes naturally to your mind.
7
Make sure you have paper and pen ready before starting the test.
8
Once started, the test cannot be paused. Make sure you have 16 minutes of uninterrupted time.

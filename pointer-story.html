Pointer Story Test Instructions

1
You will be shown 2 story paragraphs, each for 30 seconds.
2
After each story, you'll have 3 minutes and 20 seconds to write your own story based on what you saw.
3
Read each story carefully and try to understand the characters, setting, and plot.
4
When writing your story, be creative and include details about what happened before, during, and after the scene.
5
There are no right or wrong answers. Write what comes naturally to your mind.
6
Make sure you have paper and pen ready before starting the test.
7
Once started, the test cannot be paused. Make sure you have 8 minutes of uninterrupted time.
